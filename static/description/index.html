Sales Discount Limit

Set the discount limit at the sales team level

Translated languages

Sales Teams

In the form view you will have a field called discount limit, where you will place the percentage allowed for this equipment

Set Discount Limit

As we see in this example, we will set a limit of 50% discount allowed on equipment sales.

Message Alert

When you establish a greater discount than allowed for your sales team, it will notify you by means of a message, indicating the limit of your sales team.

Details

-The discount limit configuration will be established in sales order lines

-The group for discount on sales order lines will automatically inherit all vendors

-Company parameters: The parameter "sale.group_discount_per_so_line" must be created with value "True"

If this setting is not established check the following:

1. After that, enter general settings, and without any changes, save, this will reset the settings.

-We also have the option of manually activating the discount for sales order lines
